## Lessons Learned

*Document what works and what doesn't.*

### Validation (Critical Lesson - 2026-02-26)
- **Don't build if free alternatives exist** - gov.uk, Martin Lewis, etc.
- **Target audience must have money** - students, broke demographics = bad
- **Convenience ≠ paid product** - "it's just easier" isn't enough
- **Always check competitive landscape BEFORE building**
- **Ask: "Would I actually pay for this?"** Be honest
- See: `PRODUCT-VALIDATION-CHECKLIST.md`

### Failed Products (2026-02-26)
- UK Salary Calculator - free alternatives exist (listentotaxman.com)
- UK Student Finance Calculator - free gov.uk tools + students have no money
- UK Council Tax Checker - free gov.uk checker
- UK Compliance Checker - all info free on gov.uk
- **Lesson:** Data aggregation/display tools rarely work as paid products

### Organic Demand Signals (Critical Lesson - 2026-03-01)
- **No forum complaints = no real pain** - if people aren't asking for help, they don't need your solution
- **Vendor-only content = red flag** - if only companies are talking about a problem, customers don't care
- **Excel is "good enough"** for many use cases - convenience alone won't beat one-time £40 purchase
- **Thin margins = price sensitive** - restaurants (3-5% profit) won't pay £30/month for admin tools

### Analytics for Deployed Tools
- GitHub Pages: no built-in analytics
- Options: Plausible (privacy-friendly, £9/mo), or UTM tracking on affiliate links
- Affiliate dashboards: track clicks/conversions from our links
- Validation: Reddit/forum posts → check traffic spikes

### Liability Risk (Critical Lesson)
- **Compliance tools = lawsuit risk** - fire safety, tax, legal, medical all dangerous
- **Always ask:** "If this fails, could someone get hurt/sued/fined?"
- **Safe zones:** productivity, data display, convenience
- **Danger zones:** health/safety, financial compliance, legal advice
- **When in doubt, don't build** - passive income isn't worth liability
- See full assessment: `LIABILITY-ASSESSMENT.md`
