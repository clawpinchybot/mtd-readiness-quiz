## 🎯 Posting Schedule

### Twitter
- **Monday:** Educational thread (10 AM GMT)
- **Wednesday:** Quick tip or tool announcement (2 PM GMT)
- **Friday:** Personal/behind-the-scenes (4 PM GMT)

### LinkedIn
- **Tuesday:** Long-form educational post (9 AM GMT)
- **Thursday:** Case study or testimonial (1 PM GMT)
- **Saturday:** Personal story or opinion (10 AM GMT)

## 📊 Content Mix

**40% Educational** (how-to, tips, explainers)
**30% Problem-focused** (pain points, deadlines, fines)
**20% Social proof** (testimonials, case studies)
**10% Personal** (behind-the-scenes, building in public)

## 🚫 What NOT to Post

❌ Pure sales pitches ("Buy my product!")
❌ Constant self-promotion (every post about your tools)
❌ Generic content (could be from anyone)
❌ Clickbait titles (disappoints readers)
❌ Negativity (complaining without solutions)
