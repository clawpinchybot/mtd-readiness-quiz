## 🎯 Product Hunt Strategy

### Why Product Hunt?
- Tech-savvy audience (developers, agencies, founders)
- Early adopters who try new tools
- High-quality backlinks for SEO
- Potential for viral exposure

### Best Launch Day
- **Tuesday or Wednesday** (highest traffic)
- **6 AM PST / 2 PM GMT** (peak hours)
- Avoid: Weekends, Monday mornings, Friday afternoons

### Competition
Check what else is launching that day:
- Avoid days with 50+ products
- Aim for 20-40 products (sweet spot)
- Use producthunt.com/upcoming
