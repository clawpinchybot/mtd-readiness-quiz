### Page 4: Step-by-Step Action Plan
1. List all your properties
2. List all tenants at each property
3. Wait for government Information Sheet (March 2026)
4. Serve to each tenant by May 31, 2026
5. Keep proof of service
6. Update tenancy records

### Page 5: What Changes on May 1, 2026
- No more fixed-term ASTs
- All tenancies become periodic
- Section 21 abolished
- New Section 8 grounds only
- Rent increase rules change
- Pets become implied right
