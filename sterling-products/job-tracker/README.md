# JobFlow - Simple Job Tracker for UK Sole Traders

## What It Is

A free, browser-based job tracking tool for UK sole traders (electricians, plumbers, builders, etc.) who are drowning in paperwork.

**No signup. No monthly fee. Works offline.**

## Problem Solved

From ElectriciansForums.net:
> "Tracking each job and what stage it is at is becoming a bit of a chore... I have all that on various Excel files but keeping track of that is becoming a bit of a nightmare... I have to manually update it all but it's easy to forget sometimes"

Existing solutions (ServiceM8, Tradify, etc.) cost £20-50/month and are overkill for sole traders.

## Features

- **Job Pipeline**: Quote → Confirmed → In Progress → Invoiced → Paid
- **Auto Job Numbers**: JOB-1001, JOB-1002, etc.
- **Client & PO Tracking**: Perfect for B2B work with purchase orders
- **Stats Dashboard**: See outstanding quotes, work in progress, invoiced, paid
- **CSV Export**: Send to accountant or import elsewhere
- **Offline**: All data stored in browser (localStorage)
- **Mobile-Friendly**: Works on phone while on site
