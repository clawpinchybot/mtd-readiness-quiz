**Trends:**

## Opportunity Candidates

### 1. UK Landlord Rent Tracker Extension
**Problem:** Landlords manually check bank statements for rent payments
**Solution:** Extension that highlights/parses bank statement PDFs for rent payments
**Competition:** None found specifically for UK landlords
**Price:** £5 one-time or £2/mo
**Risk:** Niche, limited market

### 2. GDPR Cookie Consent Auditor
**Problem:** UK small businesses don't know if their cookie consent is compliant
**Solution:** One-click audit of any website's cookie consent implementation
**Competition:** Some enterprise tools (£100+/mo), no simple Chrome extension
**Price:** £9 one-time
**Risk:** GDPR fatigue, but new regulations keep it relevant

### 3. UK Tradesman Quote Builder
**Problem:** Tradesmen manually copy product prices from supplier websites
**Solution:** Highlight any price on a supplier site, add to quote template
**Competition:** None found for UK-specific tradesmen
**Price:** £5/mo
**Risk:** Need to support multiple supplier sites

### 4. Right to Rent Document Checker
**Problem:** Landlords confused about which documents are valid for Right to Rent
**Solution:** Upload photo of document, extension checks against Home Office list
**Competition:** None found
**Price:** £3 one-time
**Risk:** Liability concerns, may need legal review
