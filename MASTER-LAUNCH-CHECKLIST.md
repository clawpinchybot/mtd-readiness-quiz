# Master Product Launch Checklist - Sterling Products

**Purpose:** Step-by-step launch guide for ANY Sterling product
**Time to Launch:** 2-48 hours depending on product type

---

## 🚀 Pre-Launch (Week Before)

### Product Preparation
- [ ] Product tested and working 100%
- [ ] All known bugs fixed
- [ ] Mobile-friendly (if applicable)
- [ ] Cross-browser tested (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- [ ] Analytics/tracking installed (if applicable)

### SEO & Technical
- [ ] Meta tags added (title, description, keywords)
- [ ] Open Graph tags added (for social sharing)
- [ ] Twitter card tags added
- [ ] Canonical URL set
- [ ] Screenshot created (1200x630px)
- [ ] Privacy policy page created (if collecting data)

### Marketing Materials
- [ ] Reddit post written (see `/marketing/REDDIT-POSTS.md`)
- [ ] Twitter posts written (see `/marketing/SOCIAL-MEDIA-CALENDAR.md`)
- [ ] LinkedIn post written
- [ ] Email outreach templates ready (if B2B)
- [ ] Product Hunt listing prepared (if applicable)

### Pricing & Payment
- [ ] Price set (see `/PRICING-STRATEGY.md`)
- [ ] Payment processor configured (Stripe/Gumroad/etc.)
- [ ] Discount codes created (if applicable)
- [ ] Refund policy defined

---

## 📦 Launch Day (Morning)

### Final Checks
- [ ] Product deployed and accessible
- [ ] All links working
- [ ] Payment flow tested (do a test purchase)
- [ ] Contact/support method visible
- [ ] Mobile experience checked on actual device

### Social Media Launch
- [ ] Post to Reddit (Tuesday-Thursday 9-11 AM UK time)
  - See `/marketing/REDDIT-POSTS.md` for copy
  - Engage with comments within 30 minutes
- [ ] Post to Twitter (2-3 tweets throughout day)
  - Morning: Announcement
  - Afternoon: Problem/solution
  - Evening: Social proof
- [ ] Post to LinkedIn (9 AM GMT)
  - Long-form educational post
- [ ] Share in relevant Facebook groups (if applicable)

### Email Outreach (if B2B)
- [ ] Send 10-20 cold emails to target customers
  - See `/marketing/EMAIL-TEMPLATES.md`
- [ ] Track opens and responses
- [ ] Prepare follow-up emails for tomorrow

---

## 📊 Launch Day (Afternoon/Evening)

### Monitoring
- [ ] Check analytics every 2 hours
- [ ] Respond to ALL comments on Reddit/Twitter/LinkedIn
- [ ] Answer customer questions within 1 hour
- [ ] Track sales/signups in real-time

### Engagement
- [ ] Thank people for feedback
- [ ] Address any criticism constructively
- [ ] Collect testimonials (if someone says something nice, ask to quote them)
- [ ] Note feature requests for roadmap

---

## 🎯 Product Hunt Launch (If Applicable)

### Pre-Launch (1 Week Before)
- [ ] Create Product Hunt teaser page
- [ ] Reach out to 5-10 hunters
- [ ] Gather 10-15 supporters for launch day
- [ ] Create gallery images (2-5 images, 1270x760px)
- [ ] Record demo video (30-60 seconds)
- [ ] Write maker comment

### Launch Day (6 AM PST / 2 PM GMT)
- [ ] Product goes live on Product Hunt
- [ ] Post maker comment immediately
- [ ] Share on Twitter/LinkedIn with PH link
- [ ] Email supporters with direct link
- [ ] Respond to ALL comments within 30 minutes
- [ ] Engage with other Product Hunt launches

### Product Hunt Listing
- [ ] Tagline: 60 characters max
- [ ] Description: 260 characters max
- [ ] Topics: 5 relevant topics
- [ ] Gallery: 2-5 images
- [ ] Video: 30-60 seconds
- [ ] Maker comment: Posted immediately

See full guide: `/marketing/PRODUCT-HUNT-LAUNCH-KIT.md`

---

## 📈 Week 1 Post-Launch

### Daily Tasks
- [ ] Check analytics (traffic, conversions)
- [ ] Respond to all customer emails within 24 hours
- [ ] Engage on social media (reply to mentions)
- [ ] Track sales/signups

### Marketing Continuation
- [ ] Post on Reddit (different subreddits, spacing 2-3 days)
- [ ] Continue Twitter posting (3-5 times per week)
- [ ] Continue LinkedIn posting (2-3 times per week)
- [ ] Send follow-up emails (if cold outreach)
- [ ] Share in relevant communities/forums

### Data Collection
- [ ] Track conversion rates
- [ ] Collect user feedback
- [ ] Note common questions/objections
- [ ] Document feature requests
- [ ] Gather testimonials

---

## 📊 Week 2 Post-Launch: First Review

### Analytics Review
- [ ] Total traffic (vs goal)
- [ ] Conversion rate (vs goal)
- [ ] Revenue (vs goal)
- [ ] Top traffic sources
- [ ] User behavior (time on site, bounce rate)

### Decision Framework

**Kill Signals:**
- ❌ <100 visitors after marketing
- ❌ <1% conversion rate
- ❌ No revenue after 2 weeks
- ❌ Overwhelming negative feedback

**Keep Signals:**
- ✅ 100+ visitors
- ✅ 1-3% conversion rate
- ✅ Some revenue
- ✅ Positive feedback with feature requests

**Scale Signals:**
- ✅ 500+ visitors
- ✅ 3%+ conversion rate
- ✅ Consistent revenue
- ✅ Strong positive feedback
- ✅ Viral/word-of-mouth growth

### Next Steps

**If Kill:**
- [ ] Take down product or make free
- [ ] Document lessons learned
- [ ] Move to next opportunity

**If Keep:**
- [ ] Continue marketing
- [ ] Implement top feature requests
- [ ] Optimize conversion rate
- [ ] Review again in 2 weeks

**If Scale:**
- [ ] Increase marketing budget (if applicable)
- [ ] Add new features
- [ ] Consider price increase
- [ ] Explore partnerships/affiliates

## 🚫 Common Launch Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Don't:**
- Launch on Friday or weekend
- Ignore comments/questions
- Overpromise features
- Price too low (signals low quality)
- Skip testing on mobile
- Forget to track analytics
- Give up after 1 day

✅ **Do:**
- Launch Tuesday-Thursday
- Respond to everyone within 1 hour
- Underpromise, overdeliver
- Price based on value delivered
- Test on multiple devices
- Set up tracking before launch
- Give it at least 2 weeks
